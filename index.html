MV
🗹 New York City  →  🌼 Los Angeles

“The piece should feel like armor the moment you put it on.”

Molten Vice came out of a perspective that only develops when you’ve lived in two cities at once — carrying the edge and relentlessness of New York into the openness and visual culture of Los Angeles. Five years after making the move west, that tension is still what drives every piece we build.

New York taught us that craft has to be unimpeachable — the city will tell you immediately when something isn’t right. LA opened up what that craft could look like: more expressive, more personal, more intentional. Molten Vice is what happens when you stop choosing between those two things.

We work with real gold across multiple karat weights, paired with moissanite, hand-set CZs, and genuine diamonds. Every set begins as a conversation and ends as a piece you’ll never see anywhere else. No assembly line. No duplicates. One set at a time.
