MV

"The piece should feel like armor the moment you put it on."

Molten Vice was born from a simple conviction: custom jewelry should be as individual as the person wearing it. Based in Los Angeles, we operate as a bespoke grillz studio — obsessive about aesthetics, relentless in our craft.

We work with gold, white gold, and rose gold across multiple karat weights, paired with stones ranging from moissanite to hand-set CZs and genuine diamonds. Every set begins as a conversation and ends as a piece you'll never see anywhere else.

This isn't production work. There's no assembly line. Just focused, intentional craftsmanship — one set at a time.
